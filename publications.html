CARMA II: A Ground Vehicle for Autonomous Surveying of Alpha, Beta, and Gamma Radiation

Abstract: This manuscript discusses the development and capabilities of CARMA II, a ground vehicle designed for autonomous surveying of alpha, beta, and gamma radiation. The research highlights the vehicle's ability to safely navigate hazardous environments and gather important radiation data for use in various scientific and industrial applications.
