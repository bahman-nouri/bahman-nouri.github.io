About Me

Hello! I am Bahman Nouri, a Lecturer in Robotics at The University of Manchester. My research focuses on teleoperated and (semi-)autonomous robots for medicine and scientific exploration. I have extensive experience in both academic and industrial projects that involve robotic systems, and I am passionate about advancing this field through innovative solutions and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Opportunities

Open opportunities for Ph.D. and Postdoctoral Research Associates in my group are listed on LinkedIn and the University Job Board. Prospective MSc students interested in Robotics research should refer to the Robotics MSc program or contact me directly for specific project opportunities.

Research

My work has focused on the development of teleoperated and/or (semi-)autonomous robots applied to medical and scientific exploration.

  • Pediatric surgery: ICRA19, RAL20, T-MRB21, and JMRCAS23
  • Eye surgery: T-MRB22 and ICRA23
